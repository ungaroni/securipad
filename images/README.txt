Drop image and video files into the matching subfolder.
Then open content.js and change the URL/path to point at your local file.

Example:
  Before:  image: "https://www.compostor.co.il/wp-content/.../revadim.jpg"
  After:   image: "images/farms/revadim.jpg"

Folders:
  hero/            Hero photo (right column of homepage top)
  product/         The four product-tab photos
  science/         The three Science cards (photos OR short MP4 videos)
  farms/           The 8 farm cards
  testimonials/    Portrait photos for the Testimonial section (when enabled)
  sustainability/  Optional photos for the sustainability section
